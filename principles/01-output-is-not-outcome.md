# Output Is Not Outcome

## Principle

**Output is not outcome.**

Code written, pull requests merged, deployments made, and backlog items completed are outputs. Outcomes describe what changed in the business, the product, the customer experience, or the strategic position of the company as a result.

## Why this matters

Teams usually have strong tools for measuring output:
- pull request counts
- deploy counts
- cycle time
- story points
- generated code volume

Those measures are easy to capture, but they do not prove customer benefit, business traction, strategic movement, or lower risk.

## Summary

VISTA starts from the idea that engineering performance should be judged by the outcomes it creates, not just the activity it makes visible.
