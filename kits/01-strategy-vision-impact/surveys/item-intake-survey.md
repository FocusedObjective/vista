# AskPilot Survey Draft: Item Intake and Impact Challenge

## Goal
Challenge a proposed item before coding or urgent review begins.

## Suggested survey flow

1. What is the item?
2. Who benefits if this succeeds?
3. What meaningful benefit should it create?
4. What strategic objective does it support?
5. Why should this come before the best alternative?
6. What happens if we wait?
7. What evidence supports doing it?
8. What evidence is missing?
9. What would make this not worth doing now?
10. What questions must be answered before approval?

## AskPilot output goal

Produce:
- short item summary
- likely Value
- likely Impact
- likely Timing
- missing evidence
- approval or discovery recommendation
