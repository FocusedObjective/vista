# AskPilot Survey Draft: Team Calibration

## Goal
Surface whether leaders and teams share the same view of strategy.

## Suggested questions

1. What strategic objective matters most this quarter?
2. What kind of work should rise in priority under that strategy?
3. What kind of work should fall?
4. What capability gap matters most?
5. What would be a locally useful but strategically weak item?
6. What would be a strategically important but difficult item?
7. What signals would tell us our current strategy is wrong or incomplete?

## Use

Compare responses across leaders, PMs, engineering managers, and ICs.
Misalignment here is an early warning sign that Impact scoring will be noisy.
