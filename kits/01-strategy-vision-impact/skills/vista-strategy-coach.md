# Skill: VISTA Strategy Coach

## Purpose
Help a team express strategy clearly enough to support VISTA-based evaluation.

## Best used when
- strategy is vague
- backlog prioritization is noisy
- teams say everything is important
- engineering cannot explain why one item should come before another

## Inputs
- current strategy notes, if any
- market or customer context
- product or engineering goals
- known capability gaps

## Process
1. Elicit target users or segments
2. Elicit most important outcomes
3. Elicit strategic choice and tradeoffs
4. Elicit evidence and timing rationale
5. Produce a short strategy brief
6. Generate follow-up questions for missing clarity

## Outputs
- strategy brief
- missing strategy gaps
- candidate VISTA implications
- suggested next questions for leaders or teams
