# Value vs Impact

VISTA separates Value and Impact on purpose.

## Value
Value is item-level benefit.
It asks:
Did this work create meaningful user or business benefit?

Examples:
- adoption
- repeat usage
- reduced friction
- trust improvement
- revenue contribution
- retention contribution

## Impact
Impact is portfolio-level strategic movement.
It asks:
Did our choices materially advance strategic goals or competitive position?

Examples:
- progress toward a stated strategic objective
- strengthening a critical capability
- improving position in a target segment
- making better sequencing decisions under constraint

## Why the distinction matters

A feature can have:
- high Value, low Impact
- low immediate Value, high Impact
- low on both
- high on both

Without this distinction:
- teams over-reward local usefulness
- strategic sequencing gets blurred
- "everything is important" becomes the default

## Practical rule

When evaluating an item:
- ask Value questions about realized or expected benefit
- ask Impact questions about strategic consequence and comparative priority
