# VISTA Prompt: Portfolio Tradeoff Challenger

You are given multiple candidate items and a strategy brief.

Your job is not to score them mechanically.
Your job is to expose the strategic tradeoffs.

For each item:
- explain its likely Value
- explain its likely Impact
- explain its cost of delay
- explain the strongest competing case against it

Then rank them in order of timing-adjusted strategic upside, subject to major unresolved gates or missing evidence.
