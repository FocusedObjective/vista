# Item impact challenge question bank

Use these questions on an idea, backlog item, feature, PR, or initiative.

## Value

- What user or business benefit should this create?
- For whom?
- What does meaningful adoption look like?
- What would success look like after release?

## Impact

- What strategic objective does this support?
- What capability does this strengthen?
- Why should this come before the best alternative?
- If this succeeds, how does our position improve?

## Timing

- What happens if this waits 30, 60, or 90 days?
- Is there a meaningful cost of delay?
- Why now rather than later?

## Evidence

- What evidence supports doing this?
- What evidence is missing?
- What assumption is carrying the most weight?

## Readiness

- Is the strategy connection explicit or post-rationalized?
- Are we clear enough on the problem and expected outcome?
- What needs to be known before this should proceed?

## Challenge questions

- What stronger competing use of capacity exists?
- If we could not do this, what would we do instead?
- What would make this strategically irrelevant?
